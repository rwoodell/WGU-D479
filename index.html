Welcome to Taniti

About The Island

Taniti is a tropical island in the Pacific. Although the island is small, having an area of less than 500 square miles, Taniti boasts lush rainforests, varied, mountainous terrain, and an active volcano. The island also has beautiful sandy and rocky beaches and a small but safe harbor. Taniti has an indigenous population of about 20,000. Until recently due to an influx in tourism, the island's economy was dominated by fishing and agriculture.

From the tall forest peaks and the emerald waters, to the variety of island cuisine, culture, and entertainment, to relaxing hotels and tours, Taniti prides itself in being a paradise among the Pacific.
