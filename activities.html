Things To Do

Activities

Want a break away from the sights, something to spice up your vacation, or events you can enjoy with your family and loved ones? In and around Taniti City, there are a plethora of arcades, game centers, museums, and brewerys to see, so you won't miss out on the festivities during your stay.



Helicopter Rides

Get an aerial view of the rainforests, seas, and volcano all in one by taking one of our helicopter tours.



Museums

Immerse yourself in the rooted history and culture of Taniti by visiting our history museum, located in central Taniti City.



Golf Course: Coming Soon!

We are currently working on a nine-hole golf course, which should be operational some time in the next year.

We also have arcades, brewerys, and bowling!
