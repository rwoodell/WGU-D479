The Waters Of Taniti

Oceans and Beaches

Taniti island boasts beautiful emerald waters and miles of beaches, perfect for relaxing or enjoying a vacation. Near Taniti City is Yellow Leaf Bay, which is known for its pristine white, sandy beaches. In addition, there are also magnificent rocky beaches dotted around the island. Taniti's oceans are also abundant in aquatic wildlife and reefs. The deep waters and waves are perfect for deep sea diving, swimming, or surfing.



Deep Diving

Taniti's clear waters are perfect for taking a swim or snorkeling among the reefs. The abundant and colorful sea life of the island is available for all to see.



Tours At Sea

We offer chartered fishing tours for fishing enthusiasts, as well as boat tours of many sights around the island!



Beaches

Encircling Taniti City, Yellow Leaf Bay is a renowned relaxation spot for those who want to soak in the sea and sun.

Stay Safe

To ensure a safe stay, please follow proper safety procedures and stay alert for warnings, weather alerts, and swimming restrictions.
