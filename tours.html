Explore The Island

Tours

Getting around the landscape of Taniti and sightseeing everything may feel like a daunting task. Thankfully, we offer many types of chartered tours, from exploring the forests to the oceans, teaching you about our culture, legacy, and taking a look into the lesser known natural wonders that dot the island and its oceans.



Forest Tours

There are many historical and cultural sites around the forest of Taniti. We'll get to tour many sections of the forest, learning about the native residents, our roots, and about the local plant and animal life.



Fishing Tours

Whether or not it's your first time fishing, our fishing tours are available to everyone. You'll get to spend some time out at sea and on the rivers, learning about the local aquatic wildlife and getting the chance to get a catch of the day.



Sea Tours

Our sea tours take you on a journey around the perimeter of the island. You'll learn all about what lies beneath the waves, including about the local aquatic wildlife and the coral reefs. There will be some stops around Taniti City and some historical sites near the shore.
