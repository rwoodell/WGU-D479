More About Taniti

Frequently Requested Information

Power Outlets

Power outlets are 120 volts - the same as in the United States.

Alcohol Restrictions

The legal drinking age for Taniti is 18, and is not strictly enforced. Also, alcohol is not allowed to be served between the hours of midnight and 9:00 AM.

Language Barriers

Many younger Tanitians and people living in urban areas speak fluent English. However, English is spoken much less frequently in rural areas, especially by older residents.

Medical Care

Taniti has one hospital and several clinics. Language barriers at these locations should not be an issue as there are many multilingual employees.

Crime Rate

Although violent crime is very rare on Taniti, due to the influx in tourism, there has been more reports of pickpocketing and other petty crimes.

Business Hours

Though businesses may be closed seemingly unexpectedly, Taniti actually observes many national holidays. Visitors should plan their trip accordingly.

Currency

The national currency of Taniti is the U.S. Dollar. However, many businesses will also accept euros and yen. Several banks facilitate currency exchange, and many businesses accept major credit cards.
