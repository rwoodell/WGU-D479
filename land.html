Natural Wonders

Volcano And Rainforests

Taniti is home to a diverse, flourishing landscape of plant and animal life. The tropical rainforests span many miles and are perfect for adventurers and explorers. The island also boasts mountainous, rugged terrain for those up to the challenge. Taniti has one volcano, which lies at the heart of the island, and is still active to this day.



Forests

The rainforests of Taniti are home to many unique species of plant and animal life for nature lovers and sightseers alike to explore.



Mountains

Tall, forested mountains offer a perfect challenge for mountain climbers, hikers, and sightseers.



Volcano

The volcano that shaped the island of Taniti is still active to this day, constantly reshaping the landscape. Climbing the volcano offers a one of a kind sight, especially before dark.

Ziplining

There are many ziplines stationed around the rainforests, offering a fast-paced and exciting way to get around the land.
